Four pillars

OK Player is built around four things, ranked — when they conflict, the lower one wins.

01

Most elegant design

Native Fluent/Mica, macOS-utility-grade refinement. A single video plane with controls that auto-hide, and a proper now-playing card for audio.

02

Best subtitle UX

Effortless loading, comfortable style presets, dual subtitles, precise sync — and captions that lift clear of the on-screen controls.

03

Chapters with thumbnails

Chapters as thumbnail + title + time, not bare timestamps. Seek-bar hover previews and your own bookmarks.

04

Screenshots & precise nav

Clean and with-subtitles capture, copy-to-clipboard, frame stepping, exact timecode jumps, and frame-accurate readouts.
